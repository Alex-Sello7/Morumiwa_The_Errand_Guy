About Us

Morumiwa Logistics Holdings, also known as Morumiwa the Errand Guys, is a specialized logistics and errand-running company based in South Africa. We understand the fundamental challenge individuals and organizations face: the impossibility of being in two places at once.

This creates a significant gap between what needs to be done and the time and physical capacity to do it, leading to lost opportunities and added stress. Morumiwa is dedicated to solving this problem.

Our Comprehensive Services

Morumiwa specializes in running errands for individuals, businesses, and hospitals. Explore our tailored services below to find the perfect solution for your needs.

Individual Errands

We provide a helping hand for busy individuals and families, handling everyday tasks and important personal appointments with professionalism.

  • ✓Core Errands: Grocery shopping, food delivery, and pharmacy medicine collection.
  • ✓Premium Errands: Attending parent meetings as a professional representative, and license disk renewal.

Business Errands

We act as a reliable partner for businesses and organizations, providing logistical support that allows your team to maintain focus and productivity.

  • ✓Core Errands: Staff transport, bank deposits, and courier services.
  • ✓Premium Errands: Attending tender briefings on your behalf and providing extra hands for deco and event companies.

Hospital Errands

We provide compassionate and specialized services for patients, their families, and hospital staff. This includes:

  • ✓**For Patients:** Collection and delivery of personal items like blankets, warm meals, and personal hygiene products; bill payments, and assignment submission.
  • ✓**For Staff:** Meal delivery and other conveniences, helping busy healthcare professionals save time.
